Quite Terribly Simple.

October 2021
FBAUL

This poster was created based on a quote from Dada Manifest, Hugo Ball, 1916: “Very easy to understand. Quite terribly simple.”.

The typography was explored trough analogue media with the goal of visually acentuating the message of simplicity and objectivity in the dadaist movement.

An animated version of the poster allows the transformantion of the words to take upon a different dinamic, enphasising the evolution of the phrase.
