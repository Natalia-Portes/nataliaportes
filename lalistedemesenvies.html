La Liste de Mes Envies

January 2023
FBAUL

Redesign of the french romance La Liste de Mes Envies, by Grégoire Delacourt.

The project focus on emphasising the emotional duality that the protaganist goes through during the book.

Each one of the 350 pages has a different layout and treatment that illustrates that especific part of the story.
